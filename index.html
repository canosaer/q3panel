Dark Moon: Enemy Space

Dark Moon: Enemy Space is a streamlined, single-player version of Dark Moon. In Enemy Space, you encounter a series of increasingly challenging enemy spaceships and must shoot them down with the weapons that come installed on your ship and random upgrades you find along the way. I've built a strong foundation for the game engine and have completed one level of the game so far. Many components of the game engine will be ported directly to the full digital version of Dark Moon.
